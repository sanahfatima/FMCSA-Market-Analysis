

\*\*FMCSA Company Census Data Analysis\*\*



\*\*Overview\*\*



This repository contains an exploratory and analytical deep dive into the FMCSA Company Census dataset, a publicly available dataset maintained by the U.S. Department of Transportation. The analysis focuses on understanding the structure, distribution, and operational characteristics of registered motor carriers across the United States.



The goal of this project is to extract meaningful insights from raw federal transportation data using data analytics and visualization techniques, while demonstrating real-world data handling skills applicable to business analytics, policy analysis, and logistics intelligence.



\*\*Dataset\*\*



Source: Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA)

Access: \[LINK TEXT](https://data.transportation.gov/Trucking-and-Motorcoaches/Company-Census-File/az4n-8mr2/data\_preview)



The dataset includes census-level information on motor carrier companies, including:

\- USDOT identifiers

\- Company and operating classification

\- Geographic information (state, city)

\- Fleet size and driver counts

\- Carrier operation types



This dataset is widely used for regulatory, operational, and economic analysis within the transportation and logistics industry.



\*\*Objectives\*\*



This project aims to:

\- Clean and structure raw FMCSA census data for analysi

\- Explore geographic and operational trends among motor carriers

\- Analyze company size distributions and operational characteristics

\- Identify patterns that may inform logistics, compliance, or market insights

\- Present findings in a clear, visual, and reproducible manner
